Nirvana

Grunge legends who defined a generation and changed rock music forever.

Band Members

Nirvana was an American rock band formed in Aberdeen, Washington, in 1987. Comprised of Kurt Cobain, Krist Novoselic, and Dave Grohl, Nirvana spearheaded the grunge movement, combining punk energy with melodic songwriting. Their influence reshaped the music landscape of the 1990s.

Kurt Cobain

Vocals, Guitar, Songwriting

Birth: Feb 20, 1967 – Death: Apr 5, 1994

Lead singer and primary songwriter. Known for introspective lyrics, unique voice, and cultural impact. His songwriting often explored themes of alienation and angst.

Smells Like Teen Spirit (Live)

Krist Novoselic

Bass Guitar, Backing Vocals

Birth: May 16, 1965

Co-founder and bassist. Known for melodic and driving basslines that formed the foundation of Nirvana's sound. After Nirvana, he became active in political and musical projects.

Lithium (Live)

Dave Grohl

Drums, Backing Vocals

Birth: Jan 14, 1969

Powerful drummer whose energetic style became central to Nirvana’s live sound. Post-Nirvana, he founded Foo Fighters and has had a highly influential career in rock music.
