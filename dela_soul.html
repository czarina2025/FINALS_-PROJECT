De La Soul

Hip-hop trio known for playful lyrics, jazz-influenced beats, and pioneering alternative hip-hop.

Band Members

De La Soul is an iconic hip-hop group formed in 1987 in Long Island, New York. Members Posdnuos, Trugoy the Dove, and Maseo are known for creative lyrics, unique beats, and their influence on alternative hip-hop.
