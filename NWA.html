N.W.A

Pioneers of gangsta rap and West Coast hip hop, known for their bold lyrics and cultural impact.

Band Members

N.W.A was an influential hip-hop group from Compton, California, consisting of Eazy-E, Dr. Dre, Ice Cube, MC Ren, and DJ Yella. They changed the landscape of rap music with their confrontational lyrics and social commentary.
