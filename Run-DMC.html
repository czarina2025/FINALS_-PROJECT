Run-D.M.C.

Pioneers of hip-hop culture, known for their iconic style, rock collaborations, and groundbreaking videos.

Band Members

Run-D.M.C., formed in 1983 in Queens, New York, consists of Joseph "Run" Simmons, Darryl "D.M.C." McDaniels, and Jason "Jam Master Jay" Mizell. They revolutionized rap music with their unique style, rock collaborations, and streetwise fashion.
