Deftones

Atmospheric alternative metal icons blending aggression and beauty.

Band Members

Deftones formed in Sacramento, California in 1988. The band is known for their heavy riffs, dreamy vocals, and genre-blending approach that influenced modern rock and metal.

Chino Moreno

Lead Vocals, Guitar

Birth: Jun 20, 1973

Known for emotional singing, screams, and atmospheric presence.

Stephen Carpenter
